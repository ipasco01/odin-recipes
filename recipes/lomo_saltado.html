Lomo Saltado

Description

The Lomo saltado is a pride of our cuisine and an excellent dish to celebrate

Ingredients

  • 3⁄4 kilo of loin fine
  • 1⁄2 kilo of white potato
  • 3 tomatoes
  • 3 onions
  • 1 yellow chili
  • 1⁄2 to 1 cup of sillao
  • 1 tablespoon of grounded garlic
  • Chopped Coriander
  • Vegetable Oil
  • 4 serving of white rice (cooked)

Steps

  1. Cut the loin into thick strips. Then, season with salt, pepper and ground garlic.
  2. Next, cut the tomatoes and onions into thick wedges. Then, cut the yellow chilli into thin strips.
  3. The next step is to cut the white potatoes into sticks. Fry them in plenty of hot oil.
  4. Take a large frying pan to the fire. When it is hot, add a splash of vegetable oil and wait for it to start smoking. Immediately add the loin and let it fry without moving. When the meat is browned, on one side, start sautéing and flambéing. You can add a splash of pisco after adding the meat to be able to flambé (do it very carefully, as the heat can be high).
  5. Add the onions, yellow chili and vinegar. Keep sautéing.
  6. Add the tomatoes and silao. And keep sautéing.
  7. Add the coriander, stir and serve hot with the French fries and the white rice.
