Papa a la Huancaína

Description

Papa a la huancaina is a delight that you should not miss on trying. It's one of the delights of Perú's cuisine

Ingredients

  • 4 medium potatos, boiled and cold
  • 1 jar of milk
  • 6 water based cookies (no flavor)
  • Oil, necessary amount
  • 50 g cheese
  • 1 pickled chili
  • Salt
  • Lettuce leaves to decorate
  • Hard-boiled egg
  • Black olives

Steps

  1. Peel the chili, remove the center and its ribs, cut it into pieces.
  2. Put it in the blender with the evaporated milk, crackers, goat cheese and salt.
  3. Pour enough oil in a stream to obtain a dense, yellow sauce.
  4. Pass the sauce through a sieve, distribute the potatoes cut into wheels on the plates, cover them with the cold sauce and decorate with half hard-boiled eggs, lettuce leaves and black olives.
